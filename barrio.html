Barrio Fiesta

What is Barrio Fiesta?

Barrio Fiesta is our annual Filipino culture show, showcasing a skit, cultural dances, and modern dances. With nearly 100 performers, Barrio Fiesta is one of the best cultural shows at the University of Virginia. Because the core focus of Barrio Fiesta is to share Filipino culture, you don't have to be Filipino to perform - most of our performers aren't!

If you missed our interesting meeting, Scroll through this powerpoint and contact our culture chairs for any questions. If you are interested in joining whether it's in a skit, a dance, or backstage, fill out the interest form here!

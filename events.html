Hoo Am I

September 29th, 2019 11 AM - 1 PM

Garden II

Hoo are you? Hoo are they? Hoo am I? OYFA presents "HOO AM I?" to promote Standardizing Filipino American Studies (SFAS) with this month's theme of identity. This discussion follows the unique experience of being a Filipino-American with investigation into ally ship, the cultural values of the community, and our contributions. Come and help us discuss the age old question: Hoo am I?
